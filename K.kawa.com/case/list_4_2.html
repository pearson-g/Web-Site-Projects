  •  

    Could you make mould tooling for lampshade or accessory? How much is the mould tooling cost?

    Yes, It depends on drawing or sample.

  •  

    Could the products be packed in individual box or color box? Which costs more expensive, color box or color label?

    Yes.

    1.) Color box cost includes printing online cost (the Film and CTP)+box cost.

    If the quantity is less than 2000pcs, The printing online cost (the Film and CTP) is RMB400/item;

    If the quantity is more than 2000pcs,The printing online cost (the Film and CTP) is free of charge.

    2.)White/Brown box with Color label cost includes color label cost+box cost.

    The label cost is RMB1.5/PC, The total label cost of 2sides /box is RMB3.

    Basic on above-mentioned cost, If the quantity is more than 300pcs,The color box cost is cheaper than color label. We suggest that you can also use color box though the quantity is less than 1000pcs.
